# Legal Notice and Privacy Policy 2.2.6.9

This release consolidates reports for the same external third-party service without discarding later reports. The privacy policy retains every supplying module and its specific usage and data categories, preserves differing provider details, and treats conflicting country information conservatively for third-country transfers. The privacy-notice contract now supports stable service identifiers, with URL matching as a compatibility fallback.

Wikimedia, Wikidata, Wikipedia, and Wikimedia Commons are deliberately consolidated under “Wikimedia Foundation (Wikidata and Wikipedia)” with one Wikimedia privacy notice.

Third-party service details now appear in the order webtrees core services, map providers, external transcription providers, and additional services, followed by third-country transfers. The Gravatar entry also explains that `hh_legal_notice` may use the service to display an optional representative image.

The data-export and shortened version-check sections now appear directly before the hosting section.

The release also fixes detection of `hh-family-trees-list`, so configured per-tree research purposes are shown correctly in the module settings, and consistently uses “webtrees-Verwaltungsmenü” in the German version-check information.
